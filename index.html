Who Are We

Horozas is a third-generation family business with deep roots in textile craftsmanship.

Established over 60 years ago in Denizli, Turkey, we have evolved into a modern, internationally operating company, now expanding into Spain.

Our dual headquarters in Turkey and Spain allow us to seamlessly manage production, sales, and logistics, ensuring fast delivery and exceptional service.

Frequently Asked Questions

How fast can you deliver hospitality textiles?

We deliver our products within 7 to 14 business days, including production time. Our efficient logistics and express shipping ensure that your order reaches you as quickly as possible.

How do you ensure the quality of your products?

At Horozas, we follow a strict quality control process for every order. Each product undergoes rigorous testing to meet high standards of durability, comfort, and elegance, ensuring a premium experience for your guests.

Why should we choose Horozas over local Spanish textile producers?

We provide competitive pricing while maintaining top-tier quality. Our dual headquarters in Turkey and Spain allow us to manage production efficiently and offer express shipping, making us a cost-effective and reliable alternative to local suppliers.

What materials are used in your products?

All our textiles are made from 100% cotton, ensuring superior softness, breathability, and durability. We prioritize high-quality materials to enhance the comfort and longevity of our products.

Do you offer custom branding for hospitality businesses?

Yes! We offer customization options such as embroidered or woven logos to help hotels, resorts, and spas create a branded experience for their guests.

What regions do you serve?

We currently supply hospitality textiles to businesses in Turkey, Eastern Europe (including Bulgaria and Serbia), and Spain. As we continue to grow, we aim to expand our reach further across Europe.
