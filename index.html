Who Are We

Horozas is a third-generation family business with deep roots in textile craftsmanship.


Established over 60 years ago in Denizli, Turkey, we have evolved into a modern, internationally operating company, now expanding into Spain.


Our dual headquarters in Turkey and Spain allow us to seamlessly manage production, sales, and logistics, ensuring fast delivery and exceptional service.
